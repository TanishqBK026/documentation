Aadhaar

Aadhaar is a 12-digit unique identity number that can be obtained voluntarily by the citizens of India and resident foreign nationals who have spent over 182 days in twelve months immediately preceding the date of application for enrolment, based on their biometric and demographic data
Requirements for Aadhaar card:
1.Proof of Identity (POI) Documents , Comprising Name and Photograph.
2.Proof of Date of Birth (DOB) Documents.
3.Proof of Relationship (POR) Documents , Comprising Details with Head of Family if Required.
4.Proof of Address (POA) Documents for Aadhar Card , Comprising Name and Address.
