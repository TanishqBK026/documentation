Passport

A passport is an official travel document issued by a government that contains a given person's identity. It enables its holder travel to and from foreign countries and to access consular assistance while overseas. The document certifies the personal identity and nationality of its holder.
Requirements for Passport:
1.Photo passbook of running bank account in any public sector bank, private sector bank and regional rural banks.
2.A voter ID card.
3. Aadhaar card.
4.Electricity bill.
5.Rent agreement.
6.Driving license.
7.PAN card.
8.Landline or postpaid mobile bill.
9.Proof of gas connection.
10.Spouse's passport copy (First and last page of the passport that includes the details of the family and mentions applicants name as the spouse of the passport holder).
11.Certificate from employer of reputed companies on letterhead.
12.Income Tax assessment order.
13.School leaving certificate.
14.Birth certificate issued by the Municipal Corporation.
15.Policy Bond issued by the Public Life Insurance Corporations/Companies having the DOB of the holder of the insurance policy.
