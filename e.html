Death Cirtificate

An official document signed by a doctor that states the time and cause of somebody's death. Requirements for Death Cirtificate:
1.Application form.
2.Ration card.
3.Passport size photo of the deceased.
4.Proof of death of the person.
5.An affidavit, if registering death after one year of its occurrence.
